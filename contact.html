Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours? +

We're open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST. Our customer support team is available 24/7 through email and our online chat system.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries? +

We typically respond to email inquiries within 2-4 business hours. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours. For urgent matters, please call our priority support line.

Do you offer technical support? +

Yes! We provide comprehensive technical support for all our products and services. Our technical team is available via phone, email, and live chat. We also offer remote assistance when needed.

Can I schedule a consultation? +

Absolutely! We offer free 30-minute consultations to discuss your needs. You can schedule one by calling us directly or using our online booking system. We're happy to meet in person, over the phone, or via video call.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and for enterprise clients, we can arrange invoicing with NET 30 payment terms. We also accept cryptocurrency payments for certain services.

Do you have a refund policy? +

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most of our services. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. Custom work and consulting services have different terms which we'll discuss upfront.
